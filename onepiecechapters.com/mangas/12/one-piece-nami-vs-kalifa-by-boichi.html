One Piece - Nami vs Kalifa by Boichi

Nami vs. Kalifa is a one-shot spinoff created by manga illustrator Boichi, who previously worked on Roronoa Zoro Falls Into the Sea and One Piece episode A. It is the third installment of the One Piece Cover Comic Project.
